What is Time v3?

Time v1 is to look up into the sky and see how the day or night is progressing.

Time v2 is what man created to fit time into mechanical timekeeping machines for navigation, train schedules, and global coordination. Time v2 is industrial time.

Time v3 is the computer-enabled upgrade, measuring natural events accurately and simply to enable users to re-align their timekeeping and local coordination with nature. Time v3 is post-industrial time.
